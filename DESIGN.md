# Upkeep Design Direction

Visual strategy: Cleanroom Pipeline clarity with Living Ledger brand warmth.

Typography: Bricolage Grotesque for display and Manrope for body. The display type should feel engineered but human, with large balanced headlines and compact labels used sparingly.

Color: warm paper neutrals, ink green, restrained blue, and amber only for review states. Use tinted neutrals rather than pure black or pure white.

Motion: purposeful document-to-record transformations, subtle reveal motion, and scroll-driven step changes. Avoid glitch effects, bounce easing, and showing the next workflow step before it is active.

Layout: generous cinematic spacing, few high-impact panels, asymmetric hero composition, no nested cards.
