Recommendation

Start with Cleanroom, borrow the soul from Living Ledger.

Cleanroom is the clearest conversion path for a real product site. Living Ledger gives the brand memorability. The strongest final site likely uses Cleanroom structure, Living Ledger logo moments, and a restrained Observatory hero accent.
