# Upkeep Product Context

Upkeep is a Vision OCR-powered document preparation app for business owners and operators who need financial records organized quickly without learning complex accounting software.

Users upload receipts, bank statements, work orders, and handwritten notes. Upkeep reads the document, extracts useful fields, organizes the record, and flags uncertain details for review.

Brand voice: capable, calm, fast, precise, approachable.

Primary promise: clean records in your hands in seconds.

Avoid: overusing the word bookkeeping, dense software language, generic AI claims, and implying the OCR model is custom when the product uses Vision OCR throughout.

Register: brand.
